  Fox Demons [子狐妖怪]

From left: Shippo, Ippou [いっぽう], Jippou [じっぽう], Sanpou [さんぽう], Shihou [しほう] and Gohou [ごほう. Also known as Oyabun, Oyabin, Oyapyon, Myo and Mew. Fox demons who were separated from their parents and always bullied by Naraku and other demons. Under a misunderstanding, they thought Shippo was the leader of Inuyasha-gang and could invoke the Wind Scar. They wanted the 'Great Fox Demon Shippo' to teach them a powerful skill. Shippo was forced to trick Mizuki, and this led to Shippo's new skill, Heart Scar [心の傷], which he passed on to the fox demons.

  Hakushin [白心上人]

A great priest from over a hundred years ago who became a Buddha incarnate or a saint [聖者]. His mummified body [木乃伊] was enshrined in a small shrined on Hijiri Island [聖島]. Protected Bankotsu in one of his battles with Inuyasha with his iron club [独鈷]. When he was alive, he saved many people without hesitation. Yet, even when he was sick and dying, all that the people could think off was that he couldn't help them anymore. To save the poeple, he was mummified to become a saint. People started to hope for his death. Fear, regret and a will to live appeared in his heart. Naraku appeared to understand his fears, and asked him to come away with him instead. Created a holy barrier around Mount Hakurei [白霊山] and Hijiri Island, under Naraku's request.

Voice ActorMATSUOKA Fumio [松岡文雄]
Inuyasha's Jar#113
SightingsEpisodes 112, 113, 119 & 120. His soul was released after Kikyo had a long talk with him.
