Where to get Inuyasha anime in Singapore?
You can get the VCDs at Poh Kim chainstores, most Popular bookstores and Sim Lim Square. The last box set 13 [ep156~167] is out! The price is usually $26.95. They have dual audio tracks, one in Japanese and the other in Mandarin. The subtitles are in English. Also, movies 2 & 3 are available in these stores too.

Offers for VCDs
If you buy box set 10~13 at Giant in Marine Parade, you'll get a A3 poster of Inuyasha free. Now at Popular, you can buy one set and get the second set at half price. If you get the last two sets in Sim Lim Square, you'll get a Inuyasha bag.

DVDs
Right now, Inuyasha is only sold in VCDs and not DVDs. Who knows why?!
