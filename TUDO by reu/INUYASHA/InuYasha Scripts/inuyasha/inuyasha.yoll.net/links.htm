Entertainment

EAR TWEAK: A personal favorite. Loads of hilarious spoofs and digs at INU-gang. Check out the File it under Keh! section! Really! But make no mistake, this web master lurrrrves the INU-gang. Just the trove of manga scans and translations would make you kowtow. Or sit.

THE PUPPY FILES: Well-thought out website. Check out the essays that the web mistresses wrote. Has a nice article comparing the differences between the manga and anime versions of Inuyasha.

Inu-Goya > International Dub: Wonder what Jaken would sound like in German? Or Sesshomaru in Italian? Check out this page. It's hilarious!

WONDERLAND: A very funny website with original content. Just the Ask Fluffy section only, under Fans drop menu, is worth the visit! (Fluffy is an affectionate name for Sesshomaru) Be sure to check out all the articles under the Entertainment section!

Information

INUYASHA INFO FROM JAPAN: Informative website of all things related to Inuyasha. Includes movies and games trailers. A rare gem!

LUSH: A Inuyasha shrine with tons of info. For those who can't stand reading our scripts, this site has good episode summaries. The characters section are divided into major/minor, and are succint.

Inu-Goya: Information on the series itself, like airing schedule and album release.

Inuyasha Wikicity: This is a community in Wikipedia (a popular online encyclopedia) where everyone can edit. The information found here are detailed, and is still growing. So head over if you can contribute what you know about Inuyasha!

Anime News Network: Lots of info about the production of Inuyasha.

Youkaimura: Not Inuyasha-related, but useful for learning about the demons and monsters in Japanese culture. Inuyasha uses alot of these archetypes.

Sources

Image Gallery : TONS of image scans from Inuyasha DVDs, manga and peripherals.

Feelin' Fluffy: Check out 'Inu Soup' for souplets which are screencaps with short scene summary. Way better than episode summaries.
