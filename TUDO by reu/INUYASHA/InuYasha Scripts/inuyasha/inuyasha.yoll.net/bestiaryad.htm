  Akago [赤子]

One of Naraku's detachments who possesses Naraku’s human heart. However, the part with Onigumo which desires Kikyo is removed, and hence he can't feel pain or sorrow. Knows what people are thinking, can see their weaknesses and able to make people suffer from sorrows and hatred. Was slain into two by Shinsen priest, of which one half became Hakudoshi, and the other with Naraku's heart.

Voice ActressKOBAYASHI Ai [小林　愛]
Inuyasha's Jar#125
LongevityEpisodes 123, 124, 125, 126, 135, 141, 144, 146, 149, 151, 152, 153, 165 & 166+167.

  Ancient Noh Mask

Flesh-Eating mask carved out of a giant katsura tree which had a fragment of the Sacred Jewel imbedded in it. Since then, it has craved flesh and devoured humans for a body that will not rot.

Voice ActorYANAGA Kazuko [弥永和子]
LongevityEpisode 11. Annihilated by Inuyasha's Tetsusaiga.
Last words"You fell into my trap. Now you won't be able to escape!"
Sacred Jewel1 fragment

Last words"Do you think you can destroy me?"

  Bear

A big bear imbued with one of Naraku's Sacred Jewel to create a rampage, so as to lure Inuyasha and gang into a trap.

LongevityEpisode 39. Slashed by Tetsusaiga, then killed by Naraku's poison insects.
Sacred Jewel1 fragment

  Bear Demon [熊妖怪]

A bear demon which was slayed by Sango and the Demon Slayers six years ago at the Asashi Court. Due to the lord's greed, its carcass was not burned as told, but kept and made into commercial products. Its spirit form re-emerged to terrorize the court again.

Voice ActorSAIZEN Tadahisa [西前　忠久]
LongevityEpisode 78. Destroyed by Miroku's sutras.
Sacred Jewelnone

  Birds of Paradise [極樂鳥]

A bulbous round lump with wings, sharp teeth and a half body creature embedded in its forehead. Natural enemies of the Wolf-Demon Tribe. Dwells in groups in the mountains.

Voice ActorsElder brother: Shimura Tomoyuki [志村知幸]
Younger brother: SAIZEN Tadahisa [西前忠久]
Inuyasha's Jar#37
LongevityEpisodes 36 and 37. The leader annihilated by Inuyasha in episode 37 with Wind Scar, while the minions were slaughtered by the Wolf-Demon Tribe and sucked in by Miroku's Wind Tunnel. Also appears in 83.
Last words"Now we have two Sacred Jewel fragments! Brother! Power, we'll soon have more power!"
Sacred Jewel1 fragment

AttacksJaws of Death

  Centipede-Mistress [百足上臈]

A huge centipede that has a female torso with six human arms. Was the one to drag Kagome into the Dry Well, after sensing the Sacred Jewel.

Voice ActressIGARASHI Rei [五十嵐　麗]
LongevityEpisode 1. Annihilated by Inuyasha's Iron Reaver, Soul-Stealer. Appears in the Inuyasha Special, 147+148.
Last words"You brat!"

  Chokyuukai [猪九戒]

A pig demon who claims to be the descendant of Cho Hakkai [豬八戒] from Legend of Saiyuki [西遊記]. Abducts beautiful women against their will, and makes them carry his child.

Voice ActorSAWA Ritsuo [沢　りつお]
Inuyasha's Jar#129
LongevityEpisodes 129 & 143. Driven away by Kagome's arrow which was tied with a talisman from Miroku.
Quote"Pretty lady, will you bear my child?"
WeaponA sword. Also uses tiaras to make the abducted girls obedient.
See alsoSon Goku · Sha Gojo
back to top

  Clay Soldiers [人形兵]

Clay figurines made from human bones and ash, and infused with souls. Urasue made them as to guard her kiln. Kawaramaru modified them and infused the bones with poisonous fumes, in an invasion campaign to conquer neighboring states. The fumes will melt a person's skin and flesh, so that the bones left can be used to make more soldiers.

LongevityEpisodes 14 & 92.
back to top

  Corpse Crow

A three eyed crow that nests in corpses and controls them.

LongevityEpisode 2. Destroyed by Kagome's arrow. Also appears in episode 95. One was destroyed by Kagome's sacred arrow, the other by Inuyasha's claws. Also appears in ep121.
Sacred JewelSwallowed the entire jewel from Kagome.

  Coyote-Demon [山犬妖怪]

A demon who stole a princess's body and soul (who died from loneliness), and imprisioned her to lure men, and suck out their youth to gain strength.

Voice ActressKUREBAYASHI Takumi [くればやし たくみ]
LongevityEpisode 56. Slashed into two by Sango's Hiraikotsu.
Last words"My power… The demon power I gathered…"
back to top

  Demon Head [鬼の首]

A demon which resurfaced after Naraku's disappearance. In the past, the people of the Town of Demon Head buried her head thinking it's a charm against evil. After Naraku's disappearance, she surfaced again. She murdered the princess and took her place. Having devoured the powers of 99 priests, Miroku was the next in line, hoping to use the spiritual powers to restore her body.

Voice ActressTSURU Hiromi [鶴ひろみ]
Inuyasha's Jar#86
AttacksUses illusionary magic, in which she creates other Demon Heads as red herrings. Is immune to Inuyasha's Wind Scar.
LongevityEpisodes 85 & 86. Removed by Miroku's Wind Tunnel.
Last words"It's just starting. I'll gain another body and start over again! (...) What?!"
back to top

  Demon Worm Charmer

A charmer that places demon worms into people to control them. But when its pot is seized and placed near the victim, the demon worm would return to the pot.

LongevityEpisode 28. Slashed by Sango's Hiraikotsu.
Sacred Jewelnone (was promised one by Naraku for destroying Miroku)
