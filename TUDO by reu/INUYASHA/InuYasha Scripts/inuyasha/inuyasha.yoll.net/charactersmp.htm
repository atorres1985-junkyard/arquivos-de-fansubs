  Mayu Ikeda [真由]

The sister of Satoru, (Sota's classmate) who died in a fire. Her spirit lingers on to wreck vengence on Satoru, whom she believed to be her mother's favourite child.

  Midoriko [翠子]

The priestess of ancient times who held strong spiritual powers. The Sacred Jewel was born from a fight between her and a powerful demon. It is said that the fight between them still continues in the Jewel.

Inuyasha's Jar#26
SightingsEpisode 26. Appears in the Inuyasha Special, 147+148.

  Mimisenri [耳千里]

A demon with large long ears. Able to hear things from all around the world. Naraku asked for his help in locating the last Sacred Jewel fragment.

Inuyasha's Jar#127

  Miroku [弥勒]

Monk whose family lineage is cursed by Naraku with a Wind Tunnel in their right palms. Once a prayer bead is taken off, the Wind Tunnel sucks in everything in its path. It gets a little each year, and eventually sucks in the bearer as well. Perhaps the fear of death makes him an 'amorous' person, always asking pretty women to bear his children.

Voice ActorTSUJITANI Kouji [辻谷耕史]
young: TAKASHIRO Genki [高城元気]

SightingsFirst appears in episode 16.
Miroku & Sango episodes: 56.
WeaponWind Tunnel [風穴]. Sometimes uses his staff and charms. Charm attacks include Heaven's Punishment.
FanlistingLustful Priest

  Mizuki [みずき]

A girl who Shippo had a crush on. In a bid to prove his worth to the fox demons, he played a trick on her. Mizuki hated him since, and caused Shippo's heart to break. This in turn lead to Shippo's new attack, Kokoro no Kizu [心の傷] or Wound of the Heart.

Voice ActressTAKAGI Reiko [高木　礼子]
SightingsEpisode 130.

  Momiji [紅葉]

Part of a pair of priestesses (the other is Botan)who trained hard in the art of slaying demons and are Tsubaki's sister disciples. They were tricked by Tsubaki to fight Inu-gang so as to buy her more time. They are rather strong, but are still gigglish young girls at heart. Momiji means maple leaf in Japanese. They are native to the anime and doesn't appear in the manga.

Voice ActressKIKUCHI Shiho [菊池志穂]
Inuyasha's Jar#63 (with Botan)
SightingsEpisodes 63 & 64.

  Monkey God [猿神さま]

Monkey God who resides in a Spirit Stone. When the shrine caught fire, the three monkey spirits moved the stone to a safer place, but quickly forgot it when they found acorns. Kagome eventually helped to find the stone. Was the one who told Inu-gang that Naraku headed in the Northeast direction.

Voice ActorSAWA Ritsuo [沢りつお]
SightingsEpisode 88.

Three monkey spirits who were in charge of taking care of the Spirit Stone of the Monkey God. From the left: Buso [ブン], Keso [ケン] and Goso [ゴン]. Very mischievous, love acorns and forgetful.
