  Ongokuki [音獄鬼]

A demon that abducts children with the sound of a flute, and sells them to other demons. Abducted Rin and several other village children, and was destroyed by Reverend Ungai.

  Onigumo [鬼蜘蛛]

The thief that Kikyo sheltered. He suffered horrible burns and was paralyzed. He desired Kikyo, and offered his body to demons to gain power. Naraku was born out of this merging of demons into his body. His burnt scar is the reason why Naraku has a spider-shaped burn mark on his back.

Voice ActorYANAKA Hiroshi [家中 宏]
Inuyasha's Jar#21, #22
LongevityAppears in flashback form in episode 20, 21 & 87. Technically, his death allowed the birth of Naraku. He was cast out of Naraku as Musou in episodes 69, 70 & 71, in which he was reabsorbed again. Appears in the Inuyasha Special, 147+148.

  Orochidayu [オロチ太夫]

A snake demon who looks very much like the Fake Water God in episode 27. With the help of Gyuoh and Gamajiro, he was able to make fake Sacred Jewels.

Voice ActorNAKAMURA Daiki [中村大樹]
LongevityEpisodes 94 & 95. Was destroyed by Kagome's Sacred Arrow.
Last words"Die!"

  Oyakata [お館様]

Chief leader of the Panther Devas [豹猫族]. The Panther Devas come from the West, and had fierce battles with both Sesshomaru and his father. He was killed by Sesshomaru's father, but was mummified. The four siblings then revived him with the Sacred Jewel fragments. Very cruel leader who stooped to killing his own subordinates to replensish his soul.

Voice ActorSAIZEN Tadahisa [西前忠久]
Inuyasha's Jar#77
LongevityEpisodes 76 & 77. Reverted back to mummified form by Sesshoamru's Tenseiga and killed by Inuyasha's Wind Scar.
Last words"Give me… life…"

Inuyasha's Jar#164 (with Shippo)

Naraku's bugs from the Hive of Saimyosho. Also known as the Insect of Hell [地獄の虫]. They are very poisonous, and are the weak spot for Miroku's Wind Tunnel.

Voice ActressOHGAMI Izumi

  Priestess Sealer [巫女封じ]

A demon that eats the powers of priestesses. Takes the form of a cave, and an illusion of a child in distress is used to lure the priestesses in. A barrier is formed outside in whiche only priestesses are able to enter. The demon is immune to the powers of a priestess, and its heart lies in the center of the cave. Trapped Kikyo and Kagome.

Voice ActressVoice of child in distress: KUGIMIYA Rie [釘宮理恵]
LongevityEpisode 98. Killed by Kagome's Sacred Arrow (imbued with a Sacred Jewel fragment morphed together by Kikyo)
