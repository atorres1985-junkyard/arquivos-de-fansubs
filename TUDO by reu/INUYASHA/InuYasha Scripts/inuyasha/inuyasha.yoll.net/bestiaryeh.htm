  Entei [炎蹄]

A demon horse with hooves of fire. It is a fast runner and it could run 1000 miles a day. The legend goes that there’s a horse born once in every hundred years, and this horse died in a fire. When a volcano erupted, Entei was born. There is another legend about Entei that if you can ride on Entei, you can rule the world. Was sealed by Shinsen Priest, and released by Kagura. Became Hakudoshi's transport for awhile.

Inuyasha's Jar#141
LongevityEpisodes 141, 142, 143, 144, 146 & 149. Killed by Inuyasha's Backlash Wave when Inuyasha cornered him into a cave.
AttacksUses its fast speed for attacks. Spits fire and lightning balls. Also emits miasma.

  Eye Monster [化け物]

A reject of Naraku which seeks the Sacred Jewel to restore itself. Attacked Kikyo and Rasetsu no Kansuke for his fragment.

Voice ActorSAIZEN Tadahisa [西前忠久]
LongevityEpisode 87. Destroyed by Kikyo's Sacred Arrow.
Last words"Hand over the Sacred Jewel fragment now!"
back to top

  Garamaru [蛾羅丸]

A moth demon related to Gatenmaru. Despises Gatenmaru for eating humans directly. Prefers to feed on the despair and fears that breeds when humans are caught in the cocoons. Trapped Inu-gang into cocoons and forced them to face their greatest fears.

Voice ActorSEKI Tomokazu [関　智一]
LongevityEpisode 100. Disintegrated by Inuyasha's Wind Scar.
AttacksWields a giant hatchet. Attacks include light blades. His main attack however, is the Forest of Despair, in which humans are trapped into despair-inducing cocoons.
Last words"Prepare to meet your doom when that happens… You’ll then be eaten by your former friends. That’s your fate. And when that time comes, you’ll finally be saved from your fear of loneliness!"
back to top

  Gatenmaru [蛾天丸]

A ruthless demon who sucks the blood of women for norishment. When fully transformed, he is a moth-like demon. His blue fur, pink bulbous eyes and orange antennae makes him look like a Sesame Street reject.

Voice ActorKOYASU Takehito [子安武人]
Inuyasha's Jar#52
LongevityEpisodes 51 & 52. Shredded by Inuyasha's claws after he turned full-demon.
WeaponWields a giant hatchet. Attacks include poison powder [毒粉], poison cocoon [毒繭] and poison twister.
Last words"So he's melted. After all, he was just a half-demon."
FanlistingPure Poison
back to top

  Genbu [玄武]

A Younin who is versed in Ninja skills. Also known as Genbu of Darkness, this Ninja Turtle-like demon is part of the four Younins serving Hoshiyomi.

Voice ActorTANAKA Kazunari [田中一成]
AttacksPuppeting a.k.a Yamikugutsu, Tortoise Shell Hell Ride and Tortoise Smash.
LongevityEpisodes 137, 138 &139. Killed by Miroku's Wind Tunnel.
See alsoSuzaku · Seiri · Byakko
back to top

  Goshinki [悟心鬼]

A psychic demon. He's one of Naraku's detachments that broke Testsusaiga with his teeth. A blabbermouth. His fang was used to make the Tokijin.

Voice ActorSATOU Masaharu [佐藤正治]
Inuyasha's Jar#43
LongevityEpisode 43. Destroyed by Inuyasha's Iron-Reaver, Soul-Stealer after he turned full-demon. His head makes an appearance in episode 44.
Last words"This half-demon…"
back to top

  Gyuoh [牛王]

A half-demon. Like his father, he is human by day and goes by the name of Izumo. Izumo is actually his father's name. Like his mother, he is a bulld demon by night. Wanted to create the Sacred Jewel to change his fate, as he wanted to lead a beautiful life and become a full-human.

Voice ActorMATSUMOTO Hiroshi [松本　大]
Inuyasha's Jar#95
NameGyuoh's name in kanji, 牛王, is translated as 'King of Bulls'.
LongevityEpisodes 94 & 95. Swallowed a lot of fake Sacred Jewels in order to defeat Inuyasha. But when the sun rose and he transformed back to Izumo, the evil aura was too much for a human to bear and he died. His top half was Izumo and the bottom half was Gyuoh.
Last words"Miss Kagome… I’m dying… Am I human yet…?"
WeaponA club with protusions embedded with fake Sacred Jewels. Also has incredible strength.
back to top

  Hair Monster [毛むくじゃらの化け物]

An useless part that Naraku left behind in the castle, when his plan to absorb Sesshomaru failed. Wants to take Koga's jewel fragments so that he can revert to his original form.

Voice ActorSAIZEN Tadahisa [西前忠久]
Inuyasha's Jar#84
LongevityEpisodes 83 & 84. Exploded after Koga kicked him from inside. (he swallowed Koga and Ayame)
AttacksWell. Basically its made of just hair, lips and teeth, it uses all of it to attack.
Last words"Finally… I've eaten the Sacred Jewel fragments!"
back to top

  Hakudoshi [白童子]

A kid born from the right half of Akago when he was sliced into two by Shinsen Priest. Right after he was born and given new clothes, he slain Rengokuki and seized his horse, Entei, and his halberd. Part of Naraku, he kills monks and priests with Kagura to find the last Sacred Jewel shard in the boundary between this world and the next.

Voice ActorKOBAYASHI Ai [小林 愛]
Inuyasha's Jar#142
LongevityEpisodes 142, 143, 144, 145, 146, 149, 150, 152, 153, 157, 158, 159, 163, 165 & 166+167.
FanlistingInnocent Malice

Inuyasha's Jar#17

  Hoshiyomi [星黄泉]

A demon who fell in love with the human Tsukiyomi. Constantly harrassed by demons for this ill-fated relationship, he sought Kaijinbo to forge the Halberd of Yin & Yang. This is so that he could protect Tsukiyomi and live with her in peace. However, the demonic weapon, made from 222 demon corpses, possessed him and made him lose himself.

Voice ActorNAKAI Kazuya [中井和哉]
Inuyasha's Jar#138
AttacksHalberd of Yin and Yang [乾坤の薙刀]. Also knows some Demon Ninja Skills such as 'Shadow Replication' in which he mades multiple illusions of himself.
LongevityEpisodes 137, 138, 139 & 140. Killed by a combination of Inuyasha's Backlash Wave and Kagome's Sacred Arrow.
Last words"Isn't this great, Tsukiyomi?"
