  Juromaru [獣郎丸]

The younger of the short-lived brothers, who are the fourth detachments of Naraku. They seek to kill every living thing around them and are very swift in their movemnts. He houses Kageromaru who sleeps in his belly and only listens to him.

LongevityEpisode 46. Annihilated by Inuyasha's Tetsusaiga.

  Kageromaru [影郎丸]

The older of the short-lived brothers, who are the fourth detachments of Naraku. They seek to kill every living thing around them and are very swift in their movemnts. He is the smaller brother who sleeps in Jurmomaru's belly. Juromaru only listens to him.

  Kagura [神楽]

One of the more permanent detachments of Naraku. Longs for freedom from him. Like the Poison insects, she is like an errand-girl for Naraku too. Since her appearance, Naraku lurks less in his baboon suit; she took over the position of the lurker and spies on everyone else.

Voice ActressIzumi Nagashima [大神いずみ]
Inuyasha's Jar#40, #121
LongevityEpisodes 39, 40, 41, 42, 43, 45, 49, 50, 60, 61, 62, 63, 64, 66, 67, 70, 71, 79, 80, 81, 82, 117, 118, 121, 123, 124, 125, 126, 135, 141, 142, 143, 144, 145, 146, 149, 150, 152, 153, 155, 156, 157, 158, 163, 165 & 166+167.
WeaponA fan with which she is able create and control wind. Attacks include Wind Blades [風刃], Corpse Dance [屍舞] and Dance of the Wind Sword [竜蛇の舞]. She also has a feather which transports her around.
FanlistingKaze

  Kaijinbo [灰刃坊]

Totosai's disciple who was banished for making evil swords. Made Tokijin [闘鬼神] from the fangs of Goshinki, but was overtaken by its evil aura. Also forged the Halberd of Yin and Yang [乾坤の薙刀] for Hoshiyomi.

Voice ActorAONO Takeshi [青野　武]
Inuyasha's Jar#44
LongevityEpisodes 44 & 45. Destroyed by Tokijin's evil aura, in a fight with Inuyasha. Appears in flashback in 137 & 140.
Last words"Totosai… Your sword isn't much."

  Kanna [神無]

Kagura's elder sister - the demon of nothingness [無の妖怪]. Speaks softly and is always expressionless. She carries a mirror that absorbs souls and repels attacks. The least irritating of Naraku's detachments.

Voice ActressYukana [ゆ　か　な]
Inuyasha's Jar#42, #120

WeaponA mirror

  Karan [夏嵐]

Third sister of the Panther Devas [豹猫族]. The Panther Devas come from the West, and had fierce battles with both Sesshomaru and his father. The four siblings tried to revive their leader, Lord Oyakata with the Sacred Jewel. They plotted to kill Sesshomaru and Inuyasha to avenge him.

Voice ActressTEPPOUZUKA Yoko [鉄砲塚葉子]

LongevityEpisodes 75, 76 & 77.
SeasonSummer [夏]
AttacksFlame attacks [火炎攻撃]
CliqueShunran ・ Shuran ・ Toran ・ Oyakata

  Kawaramaru [瓦丸]

A warrior who was killed by his followers. Upon resurrection by Urasue, the spell to manipulate the clay figurines were passed to him. He modified them and infused the bones with poisonous fumes, in an invasion campaign to conquer neighboring states. The fumes will melt a person's skin and flesh, so that the bones left can be used to make more soldiers. Wanted Enju to continue making clay figurines for him, but was refused. Unlike Enju, he has a strong will to live and to kill, to comfort his soul. Affectionately calls Urasue as his mother.

Voice ActorSASAKI Seiji [佐々木誠二]
Inuyasha's Jar#92 {with Enju)
LongevityEpisode 92. Killed by Inuyasha's Wind Scar.
AttacksMainly uses the clay soldiers that he revived. Is also able to summon 'Demon of Hell', which is a gigantic clay warrior.
Last words"I’ll have him crush you with a finger!"

  Kodoku [蠱毒]

The last remaining demon to survive at the end under the Fuko spell, which was used by Naraku to regenerate his body.

Voice ActorSAIZEN Tadahisa [西前忠久]
Inuyasha's Jar#32
LongevityEpisodes 32. Released from the Fuko spell by Kikyo's arrow, and merged into Naraku's new body.

  Kurotenmudake

One of the dried demon meat which will turn back into a demon when cooked. Shippo barbequed one with kitsunebi as it was too tough to chew. It was thus reborn and was about to attack Shippo and Kirara when Sango arrived just in time to kill it with Hiraikotsu. Another version appeared in Kagome's school and was about to attack her when Inuyasha arrived just in time to kill it with Iron-Reaver, Soul-Stealer.

LongevityEpisodes 127 & 128.
Other dried meatShironyuudou · Yumenamazu

  Lizard Demon [イモリ妖怪]

A lizard demon which doppelganged as Satsuki's brother and held her hostage for the Sacred Jewel fragments.

Voice ActorFumioki [ふ み お き]
AttacksStomach acid.
LongevityEpisode 55. Fought with Shippo. Was too weak for Inuyasha to practise his Backlash Wave, and was slugged instead. Lastly squashed by Miroku's staff. Also appears in Shippo's nightmare in episode 100.
Last words"He's for real!"
