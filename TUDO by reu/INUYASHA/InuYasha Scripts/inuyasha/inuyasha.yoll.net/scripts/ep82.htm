'Adult Swim' Synopsis

With Kagome taking too long to return due to a huge exam coming up in the modern world, Inuyasha decides to go get her. Forced to wait in present-day Japan, he discovers that it’s not quite as safe a place as he thought.

Oddities

  • It might be out of character for Miroku to touch Sango’s chest. Usually he just goes for the butt, but this would actually land him in jail, not to mention that it signifies a gross disrespect for Sango.
  • Even though Kagome’s test is a day away, and she has no clue what’s going on, she is still studying the other subjects, too. She was looking at English and Biology textbooks in the library, and Science and Japanese textbooks at home.

There I met the half-demon, Inuyasha. The Sacred Jewel fragments will make a demon stronger. In order to help Inuyasha collect all the fragments, I always travel through time to the Warring States Era. An evil demon, Naraku, tries to use the fragments for himself. To obtain Sesshomaru's strong demonic powers,

Cut to flashback from previous episode, the fight between NAR and SES.

KAG:  (narrating) Naraku lured him to his castle. Just when Naraku was about to trap Sesshomaru in his body, Inuyasha broke through Naraku's barrier and invaded his castle.

SES: (furious) Fool!!! Do you think you can run away from me?

KAG:  (narrating) Naraku escaped because he had made use of Kohaku.

KAG:  (narrating) Thereafter, he vanished without a trace…

[ End of recap: 3min 14sec ]

KAG:  (thinking) Hojo… wait…!

KAG:  Sota, come here.

Sota: (opens the door) What, sister?

KAG:  Can you keep Inuyasha busy?

Sota: (holding INU's hand, walks out of KAG's room) Okay. Brother, let's take a bath together.

KAG:  (walks to the door) Thank you! (walks back to her table and lifts her textbook) Now…

INU:   (background, yelling) Hot!!

KAG's face changes. INU bursts into the room, naked and furious, holding TET in one hand and his Fire Rat Robe in the other. He is covered with shower foam.

INU:   (looking at SOT) What was that?!!

KAG is horrified.

Sota: (runs in naked, grabs INU's arm) Now you need to rinse off the bubbles!

INU:   I don't care!

KAG:  (throws a clock at INU's head) Get out!

INU winces and SOT grimaces. KAG throws a lamp at INU and SOT cowers. She then throws a barrage of books.

KAG:  (holding up her trash bin, furious) GET OUT!!

INU:   Hey! (KAG throws the bin, INU is paralyzed) Ka…GO…ME…!

The bin rolls to the ground.

KAG:  (tearing) What was the equation again…? (falls to her knees and whimpers)


Cut to living room. GRA is reading the newspaper. SOT is in his pajamas and is drying INU's hair with a blow dryer. INU seems to be enjoying it. MOM walks into the room.

Mama: Do you want to eat dinner?


In KAG's room.

KAG:  Finally…silence…

INU:   (background) What's this…?! (runs into KAG's room, sticking out his tongue) My tongue is burning…

Mama: (walks into the room holding a bowl) I think the curry is too spicy for him.

INU whimpers and makes some sounds with his tongue.

KAG:  (deadpan) Blame yourself for being a glutton. (sighs)


A while later. It is late at night. INU is seated on KAG's bed, watching her back as she is studying. He looks serious. KAG pauses and turns to INU.

KAG:  Hey you…

INU:   What? I'm not making any noise.

KAG:  (thinking) I'll get distracted with you watching like that… (to INU) Why don't you lie down?

INU:   Don't be silly. You better study hard! We are returning tomorrow.

KAG:  (turns around and grimaces) Okay. (thinking) Is he really going to watch me the whole night? I really don't get it… Why is he always so energetic?

A while later. KAG turns around to find KAG sleeping on her bed. KAG stands and clenches her fists in frustration.

KAG:  H-He fell asleep…! (looks at INU's sleeping face, thinking) What's his problem? Couldn't stop disturbing earlier…

INU continues sleeping sweetly. KAG pause and smiles.

KAG:  (thinking) Seeing Inuyasha in deep sleep… is really a first for me… (knees next to the bed and observes INU, thinking) Inuyasha should be very tired after running around like that…

Cut to exterior view of the house. Pan upwards to the starry night sky.

KAG:  (voice-over, gentle tone) Sleep well… At least for tonight…

[ End of ACT I: 21min 36sec ]


[ ACT II ]

Daytime in KAG's class. It is math test. KAG yawns as she is taking the test. The rest of her classmates write furiously. She then closes her eyes and nods off to sleep.


At the Dry Well in Feudal era. SHI is perched over the well, excited.

SHI:   They're back.

INU, carrying KAG's haversack, climbs out of the well.

MIR:   Oh… You stayed over?

KAG climbs out of the well listlessly.

INU:   (energetic) What's wrong, Kagome? Looking so restless…

KAG:  (slurring) I had nowhere to rest when you were sleeping on my bed last night! And the test was bad… (tearing, yells) How irritating!

KAG's voice echoes throughout the forest. Pans upwards to the sunny sky.

SHI:   Nice weather…!


[ End of episode 82: Gap Between the Ages ]
