Oddities and Other Notes

References

  • Heart Scar or Wound of the Heart
    Heart Scar, or Kokoro no Kizu, is a play on Wind Scar, which is Kaze no Kizu. It can be translated as Wound of the Heart, like Wound of the Wind.
  • Oyabun [おやぶん]
    Ippou or the eldest fox says Oyabun alot. Oyabun is translated as 'boss, chief or head'.
  • Pou-s & Hou-s
    Ippou [いっぽう] can be translated as information or '一報'.
    Jippou [じっぽう] can be translated as ball of cartridge or '実包'.
    Sanpou [さんぽう] can be translated as arithmetic or '算法'.
    Shihou [しほう] can be translated as secret information or '私報'.
    Gohou [ごほう ] can be translated as defense of religious doctrines or '護法'. {POPjisyo}

Oddities

  • "I've lost my first love forever!"
    — Shippo
    Technically Shippo's first love should be Satsuki from episode 55, Stone Flowers and Shippo's First Love.

SHI:   Heart Scar!

Heart Scar, or Kokoro no Kizu, is a play on Wind Scar, which is Kaze no Kizu. It can be translated as Wound of the Heart, like Wound of the Wind.

SHI bites LIZ again, and a red glow appears around him.

Oyabun: It's greater than Wind Scar!

LIZ:   (in pain) Let me go!

SHI:   (bites again) Heart Scar!

Oyabun: Master is so great!

SHI:   Come here and join me!

Oyabun: Y-yes! Let's go, everyone!

The foxes cheer in agreement. They sprint and leap onto LIZ, each getting a spot. Myo and Mew bite LIZ.

Oyabin: (bites) Heart Scar!

Oyapyon: (bites) Heart Scar!

LIZ:   NO!!

LIZ struggles in horror as the six fox demons bite him.


A while later, evening. LIZ is unconscious on the ground. The foxes are kowtowing to SHI, who has his eyes closed.

Oyabun: You're so great, Master!

Oyabin: Oyabin!

Oyapyon: Oyapyon!

Myo:  Myo!

Mew: Meew!

Oyabun: We are glad to learn from you!

Oyabin: The Fox Magic, Heart Scar is really powerful!

Oyapyon: Finally, we've learned it!

SHI:   You can use it whenever you want.

Oyabun: We had become stronger.

Oyabin: We will go look after our mother and father.

Myo and Mew nod in unison. SHI grunts proudly.

Oyapyon: We will never forget you, Master!

Foxes: Thank you very much!

SHI grunts again.

Oyabun: Well, we will be leaving now.

The foxes stand and walk away. SHI opens his eyes and look as the foxes bow to him again in the distance. They turn and journey on. SHI closes his eyes again and starts to sob when they are out of sight.

SHI:   (crying) I've lost my first love! Mizuki!!

Behind him, LIZ slithers away into the river.


Cut to LIZ moving in a field of tall grass, mumbling to himself.

LIZ:   Damn fox demon, I won't let him go the next time!

LIZ reaches the end of the tall grass, where KAG, SAN, MIR and KIR are waiting for the demon they're supposed to exterminate. MIR notices LIZ. He holds out a talisman and pastes it on LIZ's forehead unceremoniously.

LIZ:   (shocked) What are you doing?!

Electricity surges from the talisman and shocks LIZ. He pops back into a tiny lizard with swirly eyes.

LIZ:   Why…?!

MIR:   (sighs) That was an easy extermination.

SAN:  What do you mean?

MIR prays and chants.

[ End of ACT II: 21min 3sec ]


[ ACT III ]

Nighttime in the mansion. The lord is kowtowing to INU-gang.

Lord:  Thank you very much. I'm so relieved that it's over now.

MIR:   Don't mention it! Exterminating demons is my responsibility. It's just an easy task.

SHI is sulking.

INU:   What's the matter, Shippo? Is something wrong?

SHI:   Leave me alone.

INU:   (smiles) You're the one who defeated the demon, aren't you?

SHI:   I don't care!!

A group of village girls enter the room with dinner. Heading the group is Mizuki, who brings the dinner to SHI.

SHI:   Erm… Mizuki…

Mizuki sticks out her tongue and boos SHI spitefully. She then walks out. Cut to SHI looking shocked and helpless. INU laughs heartily and shakes SHI's head teasingly.

INU:   (very amused) What did you do, Shippo? Tell me everything!

SHI glares at INU furiously. INU is shocked at SHI's fury. Cut to SHI biting INU's butt.

SHI:   Heart Scar!

INU runs around the room in pain while the rest looks on. SHI refuses to let go and continues biting INU.

INU:   It hurts! What are you doing?!

KAG:  What's wrong with them?

MIR:   (prays) I think it's better to leave them alone.

SHI:   Heart Scar!! Arghhh…! MIZUKI!!

[ End of ACT III: 22min 7sec ]


[ End of episode 130: Roar, Shippo! Arcanum 'Wound of the Heart' ]
