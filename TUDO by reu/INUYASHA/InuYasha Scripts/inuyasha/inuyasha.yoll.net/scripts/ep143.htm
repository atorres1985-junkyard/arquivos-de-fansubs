Oddities and Other Notes

References

  • Headless Chicken
    Inuyasha said that demons, unlike humans, are able to move around without their heads, at least for awhile. We would never know the truth to that statement, but in real life, there was a headless chicken who lived for a full 18months. Mike the chicken was to be slaugtered for dinner in 1945. The owner, Lloyd Olsen, used an axe to chop off its head, but missed the jugular vein. Mike the chicken survived and Lloyd decided to keep him. He fed Mike using an eyedropper into the esophagus. Mike eventually choked to death 18 months later, winning the 'Longest Surviving Headless Chicken' Guiness World Record. For pictures and the full story, read Mike the Headless Chicken's Amazing Story. {Guiness World Records}
