Oddities and Other Notes

References

  • Noh-Mask:
    A mask used in traditional Japanese plays. Noh is the oldest form of theatre in Japan. As opposed to Kabuki, which represents extroverted liveliness, Noh is calculated, slow and restrained (which probably explains why the Flesh-Eating Mask moves so slowly). As with Kabuki, all the performers are men. Masks are usually worn. More details can be found at {Inuyasha-Companion}.
  • Katsura tree:
    The mask was carved out of a katsura tree, which is a deciduous tree found in low mountainsides of China and Japan. Its dark blue-green candy-scented leaves turns from yellow to scarlet in autumn. The leaves, when freshly shred, gives off a scent, which some says smells like freshly baked cakes. The smell makes it possible for a human nose to locate the tree even in dense growth. {KatsuraC} It was a kind of laurel said to live on the moon and be visible in its dark spots. {Mythological Giant Trees in Japan}

Oddities

Difference from Manga

This episode is heavily edited from the original storyline in the manga. In the manga, a woman who received the mask as an heirloom brought it to the Higurashi shrine to be exorcised. The mask possesses her body but she was knocked down by a car. The mask then finds four teenagers and eats them (in the manga, the victims were eaten instead of turned into blobs). It gains a naked body from the teenagers’ flesh that is very slow. This story takes place between The Toad Who Would Be Prince (ep8) and the Thunder Brothers (ep9-10), which explains the absence of Shippo. The rest of the story plays out the same. {TV Tome}

KAG:  (determined look) All right… Now! (pedals furiously towards the well)

INU:   (suddenly appears and zooms in front of the well) Hold it, Kagome!

GRA stands and picks up a pail. Cut to KAG appearing at the bottom of the well, holding her haversack.

KAG:  Good! I’m back! (topples at the weight of the bag)

Cut to GRA.

GRA:  Oh, sacred rice wine! Bring her back!

GRA pours the rice wine into the well. Cut to KAG wincing at the sudden ‘rain’ on her.

KAG:  What’s this?! (smells her hand) Smells like sake!

GRA:  (peering into the well) Kagome! My prayers have worked!

KAG:  (clenches her fists, exasperated) Gramps, I appreciate your concern for me, but… (sneezes)

GRA:  Sota, do you believe me now?

Sota: (peers into the well) Oh, yeah! You are amazing, Gramps!

GRA:  Then you must train hard as the next in line!

Sota: I’m the next in line?!

GRA:  Naturally!

Sota: No way! I’m going to become a soccer player!

GRA:  "Socker" player? You’re going to punch people?!

KAG:  Never mind that! I have lots of things to bring up… Help me!

Sota: Mom wants me to become a doctor.

GRA:  A doctor? (pauses) A surgeon or a physician…? I would recommend you to become…

KAG sighs.


KAG is having breakfast. She changed her clothes and has a towel draped around her neck. Buyo sits on her lap. Sota sits next to her. Buyo sneezes.

KAG:  What’s the matter, Buyo? You’re sneezing! (smells her hair) Do I still reek of liquor?

Buyo gets off KAG in a comedic fashion.

Mama: (walks in with an uniform in her hand) Here’s you new school uniform. I’ll put it in your room.

KAG:  Thanks, Mom.

Sota: Sis, didn’t you bring that dog fellow?

KAG:  Of course not! It was difficult enough trying to lose him! (thinking) Difficult enough… Yup… Life has become quite difficult.

Cut-scene of exterior of Higurashi shrine.

KAG:  (narrating) I’ve lived at this Higurashi Shrine from the day I was born.

Cut-scene of the Higurashi family.

KAG:  (narrating) Gramps, my mom, my younger brother, Sota, and me, an average ninth grader.

Cut-scene of Dry Well.

KAG:  (narrating) We have a secret well on our premises. And demons are after the Sacred Jewel.

Cut-scene of KAG and Sota rushing to school.

KAG:  (narrating) And suddenly, I’ve had this totally “unaverage” life.

Cut to KAG standing at the top of the shrine entrance staircase.

KAG:  (thinking) Even today, I wake up wondering if this is all a dream. (looks at the Tokyo sprawl) This is reality… (clenches her fist, determined) All right! (walks)

Cut to KAG’s room. The container of Sacred Jewel shards sit on her desk. Cut to the storage room of the shrine. A package glows in an ominous pink light. Cut to close-up of Sacred Jewel shards. Cut to close up of the package glowing in white light.

[ End of ACT I: 4min 27sec ]


[ ACT II ]

Exterior of KAG’s school. Greetings of 'Morning!' are heard as the students walk into the school.

KAG's friends are called Eri, Yuka and Ayumi. Eri is the one with yellow hairband and Ayumi is the one with long hair.

KAG:  (jogs to her friends) Good morning!

Yuka: Kagome! Is it okay for you to be in school?

KAG:  Huh?

Yuka: You’ve been absent a lot lately so we called your house.

Eri:   This time, it was a strained back.

Ayumi: And before that, you had gone to the doctor for a diabetes test.

Eri:   Are you okay?

KAG:  (grimaces, thinking) Gramps… If you have to lie, make it believable!

A boy on a bicycle approaches the group. He is bathed in soft white sunlight.

Boy:   Higurashi!

KAG:  (turns around) Huh?

Boy:   (stops in front of KAG) Are you feeling better?

KAG:  (surprised) Huh?!

The three girls huddle together behind KAG.

Yuka: It’s Hojo from B-section!

Hojo: (gets off bicycle) You’re going through an awful lot for someone so young. (concerned look) You have gout?

KAG:  (bows her head in embarrassed exasperation, thinking) No…!

Hojo: (gives KAG a gift in pink wrapping paper with red polka-dots and ribbon) I can’t do much, but here.

The three friends shriek and zoom far behind KAG.

KAG:  (opens the gift and takes out a sandal) What is it?

Hojo: (riding away, bathed in soft sunlight again) Therapeutic sandals! Wear them, okay? (dreamy sparkles appear around him)

KAG looks at Hojo with a stunned ‘Huh’. The three friends run and gathers around KAG.

Yuka: (excited) Hey, Kagome! Have you been seeing Hojo?

KAG:  As if I have the time!

Eri:   But he seems to really like you.

Ayumi: What a shock!!


Cut to KAG’s classroom. She sits at her place, with the therapeutic sandals on her desk. The three friends gather around her.

Eri:   Are you the least bit interested?

KAG:  Well…!

Yuka: Kagome, do you…?

Ayumi: Do you have a boyfriend already?!

KAG:  No way!

Eri:   Really? You really don’t have one?

Yuka: Then what kind of guys do you like?

KAG:  (closes her eyes and puts her hand on her face) Well…

KAG:  (voice-over) Someone who’s not rough…who’s not spoiled…who’s not short-tempered…who’s kind…and understanding…

KAG:  (clasped hands, thinking)Yes… Perhaps I’d like someone who’s the complete opposite of Inuyasha…

KAE:  Inuyasha, stop lying around. Go out and get information on the Sacred Jewel.

Higurashi shrine. GRA walks up the staircase of the entrance and notices something.

GRA:  Huh?! A fire!

Smoke appears from the storage room. Cut to GRA rushing into the room, which in engulfed in flames.

GRA:  What’s happening?!

Mask:  Give me…a body…

(takes out some chamrs from his clothes) My predecessor several generations back was asked to exorcise this mask by a Noh actor! It was named the “Flesh-Eating Mask”! I did not know it has such enormous power! (throws some charms at the mask, which burns them)

FEM:  A body…

FEM:  Give me…a body!

FEM then attaches itself on a samurai outfit and stands up, but the outfit collapses

FEM:  A body… A body…

GRA:  Is it looking for living flesh?!

A wooden column on top of GRA splits into two from the burning flames and crashes onto GRA. GRA falls onto the ground, with wood pieces on his back.

FEM:  A body… Give me a body…!

GRA:  (struggles to get up) Damn!

FEM:  A body! (zooms towards GRA)

Cut to the fire alarm ringing.


An ambulance drives along a road. KAG walks in the opposite direction, towards home. She notices three teenagers ahead of her.

Guy 1: Hey, the fire’s at Higurashi Shrine!

Guy 2: Let’s go! (runs off)

KAG:  Huh?!


Outside the Higurashi shrine, two fire engines and two police cars are parked. A huge crowd gathers. Policemen have formed a barrier to control the situation.

Policeman 1: The fire is under control! Please do not worry!

Policeman 2: This road is closed! Do not push!

KAG:  Excuse me! Please let me through! (gets under the barrier and runs towards home)

Policeman 1: Hey, you! You can’t go there!

In the Higurashi courtyard, Mama bows to a policeman.

Mama: Thank you very much. (policeman walks off)

KAG:  Mom!

Mama: Kagome!

KAG:  Mom, what happened?!

Mama: The storeroom caught fire. Grandpa was surrounded by the smoke.

KAG:  Gramps?!

Mama: He’s all right. He was slightly injured, then fainted before be could inhale too much smoke. I’m going to the hospital now.

KAG:  (worried) Me, too!

Mama: (places a hand on KAG’s shoulder) Sota’s not home yet. Please stay home.

KAG:  All right. (thinking) A fire in the storeroom? There’s no way a fire could start in there.


Several firemen are examining the exterior of the storeroom.

Fireman 2: Watch out! There are holes in the floor.

Inside the storeroom, firemen walk around examining the remnants of the fire.

Fireman 3: (notices something) Huh? (looks at a puddle of water below him)

FEM appears from the puddle and zooms towards the fireman. It attaches itself onto the fireman’s face, who struggles to take it off, groaning.

Fireman 4: What’s the matter?


Sota runs towards KAG nervously.

Sota: Sis!

KAG:  (kneels and puts her hands on his shoulder) It’s all right! We’re all safe! Don’t worry.

Sota: (out of breath) Uh-huh.


Outside the shrine, one of the fire engines move and crashes several police cars. Cut to KAG and Sota running towards the entrance. Cut to the fire engine running over the police cars.

Fireman 5: (stands in front of the fire engine and waves his hands) Stop!

The fire engine does not stop and the fireman runs away from it. It swerves out of the alley onto the main road. Cut to outside of the shrine. A policeman points in the direction of the fire engine.

Policeman 1: Hurry! (two police cars chase the engine) Contact Headquarters! There’s an out-of-control truck!

Pans upwards to reveal KAG and Sota staring in disbelief at the turn of events.

Newscaster: (voice-over) And next on the news… A runaway fire truck on the streets has been involved in many accidents. There appear to be several injured but that has not been confirmed at this time. It is unthinkable, but at every accident site people have disappeared. It is totally incomprehensible!

Cut to driver’s seat of the runaway fire truck. FEM is driving, in its new body of black blob.

FEM:  (reaches for the steering wheel) The flesh keeps rotting away. Rotting away! (black blobs drip from the hand, which loses its shape) If I can absorb a little more… I’ll be able to mold a complete body. A little more… Oh, if only I had a fragment of the Sacred Jewel!

KAG:  And so, Mom’s going to stay at the hospital another night to watch over Gramps. Huh?

Policemen have blocked a road with cars. They wave the batons in their hands and blow their whistles. The fire truck drives towards them and slows down. In the hospital.

Sota: Please…take me! (digs the ground with his hands, crying) I have to… or Sis will… Sis will die! (stops and wails)

The ground suddenly glows and INU appears, in a heroic fashion.

Sota: The… dog fellow…!

INU:   What’s up? You look troubled.

INU:   Don’t fall off!

Sota: I couldn’t get through the well.

INU:   (laughs) You should be glad. On the other side, there are dozens of demons who love to eat little boys like you.

Sota: Huh?!

INU jumps onto the top of a truck.

Sota: Do you know where Sis is?

INU:   My nose works real good. I just have to follow Kagome’s blood scent.

Sota: Sis cut her hand…

INU:   I could smell her from the well. I’ll find Kagome and save her without fail.

CUT TO FLASHBACK of KAG’s conversation with Sota during breakfast.

KAG:  This Inuyasha is rough and spoiled and he isn’t kind at all! It’s such a chore keeping up with him.

BACK TO PRESENT.

Sota: (thinking) I wonder… He seems much kinder and dependable than Sis made him out to be.


KAG runs in one of the highest floors in a building under construction. She stops and catches her breath.

KAG:  (thinking) Not yet? Isn’t Inuyasha here yet?

FEM:  (towering over KAG) Give it to me… The Sacred Jewel!

INU:   (appears suddenly) Iron Reaver, Soul Stealer!

KAG:  Inuyasha!

Sota: Huh?!

KAG:  Inuyasha!

INU:   What a rotten trick…!

The mask emerges from its body and joins together again. It zooms towards KAG and pushes itself on her hand with the container.

KAG:  (blocking) Huh?! (falls backwards)

Sota: Sis!

KAG:  (thinking) If I don’t do something, the fragments will…! Sota, take this and run! (throws the container to Sota) Hurry!

Sota: (nods) Yeah… (runs)

FEM chases Sota.

KAG:  Sota, run!

Sota: (trips and falls, the container falling out of his hand, thinking) That mask is after this bottle. I know! If I throw it away…! (shakes his head, grabs the container) No! (continues running) It can’t have this bottle!

Sota runs. He stops suddenly as he reached the edge of the building. He turns around and stare in stupefied horror at the oncoming FEM. Cut to INU breaking out of the body with TET and running towards Sota.

INU:   (leaps and swings TET) Listen! Don’t move!

INU slices FEM into two with one sweep of TET. It breaks into four pieces and disintegrates into thin air. The Scared Jewel piece in its forehead lands on the ground.

Sota: You did it! You did it!

INU:   (swings TET over his shoulder) Yeah! Are the Sacred Jewel fragments safe?

Sota: (nods with thankful tears) Uh-huh!

The sun started to rise.

KAG:  Inuyasha…

INU:   (turns around to face KAG against the soft rays) Kagome, not hurt?

KAG:  (gasps) Huh? (nods) No. (thinking) Huh… He’s sorta nice. What’s happening? It’s just a little… but Inuyasha seems sorta dashing. (notices the sun rising) Huh?! It’s morning?! (clasps her face and shrieks) Oh no! I have a test today! (turns around and runs off)

INU:   Hey, Kagome!

KAG:  Oh… See you, Inuyasha! Make sure you return to the other dimension! Sota! Pick up those fragments and bring them home! Inuyasha! Don’t take them! (disappears from the scene)

INU stares at KAG, stupefied. Sota stands up and tugs INU’s sleeves.

Sota: Umm… (blushing) You were real cool!

INU:   Think so? (a oh-well-but-you-are-not-Kagome look)


KAG runs in her school uniform.

KAG:  (looking at the book in her hand) Yup… Scout’s honor.

Fades to bird’s eye view of Tokyo.

{ End of ACT II: 20min 34sec ]
