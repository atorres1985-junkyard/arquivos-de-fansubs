  Rasetsu no Kansuke [羅刹の勘助]

An old bandit who teamed up with Onigumo fifty years ago to plunder and terrorize towns. His eye was blinded by Inuyasha, when he tried to take the Sacred Jewel from Kikyo. When Onigumo betrayed him, he had him burned and thrown down a ravine. Having committed a lot of atrocities, he is now looking for a resting place to atone his sins. He died before he could reach Mount Hakurei, and pleaded Kikyo to carry a bunch of his hair to the mountain.

Voice ActorNOJIMA Akio [野島昭生]
LongevityEpisodes 87 & 120. Died from a combination of old age and injuries caused by the Eye-Demon.
Inuyasha's Jar#87
Sacred Jewel1 fragment

  Salamander Monster [山椒魚妖怪]

Used to be a great demon who used to ruled a valley. Was subdued and sealed in a scroll by a priest. To destroy its form, they peeled off its skin and the priest drew a portrait of the Goddess of Mercy on the scroll to seal its soul forever, never to be reincarnated. After Naraku gained a new body and released the demons from Mount Hakurei, its demonic powers were strengthened, and managed to manipulate a village of women to kill men, so as to get skin for its new form.

Voice ActressKITAHAMA Haruko [北浜 晴子]
Inuyasha's Jar#132
LongevityAppears in episodes 131 & 132. Annihilated by Inuyasha's Wind Scar.
AttacksAcid spit and tail slam. The eggs of the salamander, when swallowed and hatched in a person's body, is able to control the person.
Last words"Half-demon! I shall eat you!!"
