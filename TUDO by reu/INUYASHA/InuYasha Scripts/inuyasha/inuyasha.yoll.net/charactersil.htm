  Izumo [出雲]

A Guuji or a priest who is curious about the world and loves nature. He travels around the country to learn more about the Sacred Jewel. Like his father, he is human by day. Like his mother, he is a bulld demon by night and goes by the name of Gyuoh. Izumo is actually his father's name. Wanted to create the Sacred Jewel to change his fate, as he wanted to lead a beautiful life.

Voice ActorMATSUMOTO Hiroshi [松本　大]
Inuyasha's Jar#94
NameIzumo's kanji name, 出雲, can be translated as 'out of the clouds'.
SightingsEpisodes 94 & 95.
Last words"Miss Kagome… I’m dying… Am I human yet…?"

  Kuroro [クロロ]

A wise two-tailed cat who got along very well with the village children. Was chased out of the village by Tessu the Rat-Demon, under the pretext that she would bring misfortune to them. Even though she was seriously injured, she still looked for Inu-gang so as to save Koume and Shippo. Fierce, courageous and loyal like Kirara, and even looks a lot like her. Gave birth to three cute kittens.
