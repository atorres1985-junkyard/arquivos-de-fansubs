Inuyasha Awards

This article is translated from a Mandarin Inuyasha forum. As it is really funny, I took the liberty to translate it and added some pictures on my own. The words in blue box are my comments of the possible contenders. The original article can be found here: 犬夜叉之最. The author is 忧雪.

The Coolest Character Award: Sesshomaru

Reason: As everyone knows, Lord Sesshomaru is indeed both handsome and cool. Like an ice sculpture, even when he speaks, his lips would not move than 10 degrees. Moreover, his reticence makes his words precious, and gives an impression of aloofness. Add a cool wardrobe and character design, heheh... you know what I mean! Aloofness and beauty are Lord Sesshomaru trademarks!

Contender(s): Bankotsu

The 'Best Taste' Award: Inuyasha

Reason: Fifty years ago, he fell for a beauty. Fifty years later, he woke from his seal to fall for another beauty. Now that's taste! But, irritatingly, he cut off Sesshomaru's left arm, giving his brother, Sesshomaru, a 'Bust of Venus' look...

Contender(s): Rin and Jaken (for choosing Sesshomaru)

The 'Bastard' Award: Inuyasha

Also translated as 'The Character that Needs a Good Thumping'.

Reason: This two-timing delinquent hoards the two good girls, each with their own qualities and doesn't even make a choice. He behaves nonchanlantly and makes the audience anxious! Can't even sense the feelings between Sango and Miroku, he really needs a good thumping!

Contender(s): Naraku (for obvious reasons) and Renkotsu

The Most Beautiful Character Award: Izayoi

Note: Izayoi is Inuyasha's mother.

Reason: Maybe everyone will ask, why not Kagome or Kikyo? Let me say this: Kagome is soft on the outside but strong on the inside, while Kikyo is strong on the outside but soft on the inside. But Izayoi has a balance of both strength and softness, and possesses an incomparable class, grace, elegance, maturity and perfection. No wonder Inu-papa fell for her!

Contender(s): Kikyo, Kagome, Sango

The Courage Award: Kagome

Reason: From modern era to feudal era, how can one go on this journey without courage? In face of so many unknown demons, she doesn't run, but fights them bravely, and gets better each time. And Kagome is a modern-day girl! Also, she has the courage to say to Inuyasha, who hasn't step out from the memories of his past yet, that "I like being with you, I just want to stay together with you!" And this Inuyasha hasn't broke up with his ex-girlfriend yet! Bravo!!

Contender(s): Kohaku (for fighting Naraku in his own way, even when his painful memories haunt him)

The 'Saddest Fate' Award: Kikyo

Reason: Needless to say, everyone should know! Kikyo is already dead, but is still persecuted by Naraku. She relies on the Soul Collectors to survive, if one day the Soul Collectors were to go extinct... But the saddest thing is, even revived, she can only look and care for the person she loves from afar. And the one with her ex-boyfriend is a reincarnate of herself!

Contender(s): Kohaku, Sango and Ginkotsu

The Lecher Award: Miroku

Reason: Everyone should agree on this! Almost every time he sees a young girl, Miroku will hold her hand and asks "Will you bear my child?" This phrase is practically his catchphrase! Who can bear his 'ethical' conduct?!

Contender(s): Mukotsu

The Most Self-Contradictary Character Award: Sango

Reason: She obviously likes Miroku but doesn't say it. In general, when asked questions about Miroku, just take her answer and invert it, and you'll have the truth. When she sees Miroku flirting with girls, she still pretends not to care, but in fact what she's thinking is written all over her face. But in the end, Sango is still a girl, so when Miroku confessed his feelings, she accepted him of course!

Contender(s): Kikyo, Sesshomaru

The Lurker Award: Naraku

Reason: You don't need a reason, do you? Our little Naraku is really scheming, and every time he appears, he gives a creepy feeling. Moreover, the times he appears is unpredictable. He can appear anytime, anywhere, and quietly too... scary....

Contender(s): Poison Insects

The Most Faithful Award: Koga

Reason: Even when he knows that Kagome likes Inuyasha, he still doesn't know to beat it, and continues to pursue her blindly and faithfully.

Contender(s): Kirara

The Luckiest Character Award: Rin

Reason: How can she not be the most fortunate character around? She is the first (and only) human that Lord Sesshomaru saved with Tenseiga. He protects her and god knows how much he cares for her. What a lucky little sidekick!

Contender(s): -

The Most Unlucky Character Award: Jaken

Reason: Almost everytime in battle, he'll be knocked by flying objects or run into trouble. Even when he says exactly the same as Rin does, he's most likely to be thumped by Sesshomaru.

Contender(s): -

The Most Modern Award: Jakotsu

Reason: He turns soft when he sees Inuyasha, and everytime in battle, he grumbles about his brothers snatching his Inuyasha. And the greatest point in case is that he is actually gay in that feudal age!

Contender(s): -

The Most Cowardly Award: Myoga

Reason: When he disappears, there is only one message -- Danger, run!

Contender(s): -

The Most Innocent Award: Kanna

Reason: No matter from head, appearance and voice, Kanna is most like a child. When she doesn't speak, she really looks like a doll in her white kimono and hair.

Contender(s): Rin, Kirara

The Most Talented Award: Kagura

Reason: She creates so many tricks with her little fan, and is quite deadly with it. Creative!

Contender(s): Naraku...

Here're some awards that I came up with myself:

The Most Fertile Award: Naraku

Reason: If Miroku were to turn gay and asks Naraku to bear his child, he would probably be ectastic about how 'fertile' Naraku is. Here's a list of his 'children': Kagura, Kanna, Juromaru, Kageromaru, Goshinki, Musou, Akago, Hakudoushi (who beget Mouryomaru).

The Most Played Flashback Award

    1. When Kikyo shoot Inuyasha (winner)
    2. When Kagome first met Inuyasha by the tree
    3. When the demon exterminators went to the castle and Kohaku slayed his comrades.
    4. When Kagome was attacked by Mistress Centipede in the well.
